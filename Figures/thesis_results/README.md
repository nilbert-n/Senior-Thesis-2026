## Caveats / TODOs

- **fig5 and fig6 (contacts)**: Contact probabilities in the Week 8 NVE run are very low (max ~0.04%). The docked ASO was placed in a non-equilibrium configuration; these figures correctly show the sparse contact pattern during the production run. Interpretation should note this is a high-loading NVE run, not a equilibrated bound-state simulation.
- **fig8 (Kd)**: No entry exists for the reference `100ASO_unmod` in `kd_ranked_summary.csv`. The 100-ASO reference Kd was analysed separately in the Week 8 pipeline (see `04_binding_analysis.py`) and uses a different method. The CSV covers the Week 10 campaign simulations.
- **fig7 (binding)**: 11 of 99 free ASOs contacted the hairpin during the 3.64 µs NVE run. This reflects the high dilution of individual ASOs in the large simulation box; the mean-field Kd analysis in Week 10 uses a more rigorous occupancy-based estimator.
- **All time-series figures**: timestep conversion is 10 fs/step (LAMMPS `units real`, `timestep 10`). 1×10⁶ steps = 10 ns.
